More about Wired Nation
Wired nation was a Managedd Services Provider (MSP). Clients of Wired Nation Inc. were small to medium sized local businesses with users ranging from 1-100+ per client. Multiple platforms supported (Windows 95-XP workstation/server, Novell, Linux - RedHat/Mandrake, UNIX – SCO, Sys V). Support was provided on site in most cases.
  • Duties
  • Provide on site support/troubleshooting (off site only when necessary) for clients.
  • Provide phone support.
  • Track hours spent at clients/traveling/in office.
  • Track mileage.
  • Document troubleshooting steps.
  • Resell hardware.
  • Emergency pager duty about once a month.
